The Counting Problem The "How Many"

Scenario: Agenda Planning

Find the number of available Friday nights for dinner with 3 Dutch friends.

Result: 0 for next 2 years

The Optimisation Problem The "Best"

Scenario: Albert Heijn Bonuskaart

Combination of '1+1 Gratis' deals for the absolute lowest price.

Result: Minimum Price

The Search Problem The "Find One"

Scenario: Biking in Amsterdam

Find one specific bike among 10,000 at the station.

Result: Exact Location

The Decision Problem The "Yes/No"

Scenario: The NS Train

Can I get a coffee and still board the train in 4 minutes?

Result: TRUE or FALSE

[[Slide Notes#Problem Types]]

We encounter different types of problems every day.

1. Counting: How many possibilities? (Dinner with friends? Good luck!)

2. Optimization: What is the absolute best/cheapest way?

3. Search: Find a specific needle in a haystack.

4. Decision: The binary Yes/No question. This one is special in Computer Science.
