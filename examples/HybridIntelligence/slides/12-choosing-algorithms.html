[[Slide Notes#Choosing Algorithms for Problems]]

As developers, we are constantly solving an optimization problem: finding the best algorithm for each sub-problem in our project.

Don't use a nuclear power plant to toast bread. In software, this means don't use a complex, heavy-weight solution (like an LLM for everything) for a problem that has a perfect, efficient, deterministic algorithm.
