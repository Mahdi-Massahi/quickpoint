Ok now let's take a look at the definition of "problem" in the field of computer science.

Garey and Johnson wrote the 'bible' of NP-completeness. Their definition emphasizes that a problem is a *template* (general question) with placeholders (parameters).

Important distinction for this talk: In formal CS, we rarely use the word "Issue" as a technical term. Issues are for Jira; Problems are for Algorithms.
