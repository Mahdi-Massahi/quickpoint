[[Slide Notes#Complexity Classes (for problems)]]

We classify problems based on the most efficient algorithms we have to solve and verify them.

The complexity hierarchy shows how different classes of problems are related: P (Polynomial time), NP (Nondeterministic Polynomial time), and EXPTIME (Exponential time) are nested within each other. The relative sizes indicate the vast difference in the number of problems and computational effort required.
