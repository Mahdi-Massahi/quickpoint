Is the weather in the Netherlands an issue or a problem?

In my home country, we complain. It's an issue. It's messy.

In the Netherlands, you don't have 'issues' with rain. You have a Computational Problem! You open the Buienradar app. You look at the data. You calculate the window.

As an immigrant, I'm trying to see the Netherlands' weather as a problem, not an issue!
