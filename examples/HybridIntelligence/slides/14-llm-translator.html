[[Slide Notes#From LLM hammer to LLM translator]]

The true power of LLMs isn't in replacing classical algorithms. It's in expanding our problem space.

They take problems that used to be 'Human-Only' (full of semantic ambiguity) and translate them into a form that computers can finally start to work with (BPP).

They are the bridge between the messy real-world issue and the structured computational problem.
