[[Slide Notes#Problem Classification]]

Let's look at some examples to see how we classify them.

1. Even number? O(1). Easy to solve and verify -> P.

2. Max in array? O(N). Easy to solve and verify -> P.

3. Jigsaw Puzzle: Hard to solve (exponential/factorial) but very easy to verify if someone gives you the solution -> NP.

4. Generalized Chess: Both solving and verifying are extremely hard. It's outside of NP, in EXPTIME.
